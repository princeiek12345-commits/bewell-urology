Since Establishment

A Legacy of Trusted Care

BeWell Urology & Men’s Clinic Limited was founded with a singular vision: to bridge the gap in premium men's healthcare. Recognizing the discomfort many men feel in traditional hospital settings, we designed a facility that feels less like a clinic and more like a premium wellness retreat.

Located at the serene Topmoj luxury home opposite the Dunamis Glory dome, our facility offers a sanctuary where men can address sensitive health issues without fear or judgment. Led by Dr. Obiatuegwu Kenechukwu, our team brings decades of combined experience in robotic surgery, endocrinology, and reproductive health.
